Tribute



Ever since we became aware of Mr. P.C. Malik's illness, we hoped and prayed that this day would not come-not now, not so soon. The entire Margaret Family has been left much poorer for the loss of one of its distinguished member.



Mr. P.C. Malik
Founder Chairman
(04/05/1992-07/11/2010)

We restrain ourselves from asking why, for we dare not question God's will. Still deeply grieved that someone with so much promise, someone who represented so much hope, should have been taken from us so soon. Those of us who have followed Malik sir's journey towards education and inculcation of knowledge are left with tears in our eyes. The dignity with which he rode the waves of education triumphs and disappointments, his emergence as the Chairman of St. Margaret Engineering College and the courage and serenity with which he faced his own death - all recognize that this was no ordinary man.

He made a significant impact in the short space of time. He brought a sharp, refreshing and engaging perspective to the Margaret Family. All of us, will miss his wise  counsel, his timely and effective interventions, his calmness even in contentious debates and that frankness that was so often tempered with his inimitable dry humor.

       

Today, we bid him farewell and, even though we mourn his passing, we celebrate his life and give thanks that he honored us by sharing so much of that life with us, giving so much of that life to us.

Abraham Lincoln said it well : "Die when I may, I want it said of me by those who knew me best the I always plucked a thistle and planted a flower where I thought a flower would grow."
