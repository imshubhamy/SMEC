Our Vision



Endeavoring to become one of the top providers of engineering and management education in the country and making available the appropriately qualified and skilled manpower to the world of work.



Our Mission

The mission of St. Margaret Engineering College, Neemrana is to strive for excellence in the filed of engineering and management education by

  • 1. by creating and developing first rate technical skills
  • 2. by Instilling high standards of discipline, ethics and values among the future technocrats and managers
  • 3. by shaping world class leaders who have not only technical / managerial expertise but also initiative, confidence, creativity and zeal to achieve their goal In particular and serve humanity in general.


Success Story

Where Vision leads Mission follows. "The Success Mantra" is to dream big with dedication, hardwork and commitment to deliver at all ends - management, faculty & students. We may be small and young as compared to IITs/ NITs, but we aspire to turn our students as excellent technocrats if not with speed then with a special skin effect which can do wonders just as providing strength to a weak yarn. Today there is little gap in technical knowledge amongst the developed and the developing nations on account of free and fast flow of information and thus the course curriculum are almost common world wide and absolutely same in institutions of the affiliating university. Yet the quality of Engineers is different of different institutions. It is the culture and the infrastructure that determine the quality of the product.

The SMEC infrastructure that is coming up rapidly is a great help in providing the cutting edge in developing innovative skills of the students. The result of the University and the Placement record speaks for itself.
